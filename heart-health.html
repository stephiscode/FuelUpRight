Why Heart Health Matters

Maintaining a healthy heart reduces the risk of cardiovascular diseases, improves longevity, and enhances overall well-being.

Heart-Healthy Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Limit saturated fats, sodium, and added sugars.
  • Stay physically active for at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Maintain a healthy weight and monitor your BMI.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.
  • Manage stress through meditation, relaxation, and healthy sleep patterns.
